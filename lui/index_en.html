  Research Interests
  Interface between artificial intelligence, computer science and operations research; Computational intelligence; Smart computing and green computing; Adaptive meta-heuristics for solving large-scale real-world and theoretical combinatorial optimization and constrained satisfaction problems:
  Algorithms/Approaches:  Tabu Search, Iterated Local Search, Memetic Algorithm, Hybrid Evolutionary Algorithm, Scatter Search, GRASP, Path-Relinking, Hybrid Exact/Heuristic Methods, etc.
  Real-World Applications: Timetabling, Personnel Rostering, Task Scheduling, Packing, Frequency Assignment, Vehicle Routing, Traffic Grooming, Wavelength Assignment, Resource Allocation, Facility Allocation, Community Structure Identification.
  Classical NP-Hard Problems: SAT, CSP, Graph Coloring, TSP, Unconstrained Binary Quadratic Programming, Maximum Diversity Problem, Protein Folding, etc.
